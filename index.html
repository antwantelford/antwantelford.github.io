Thank you for visiting our website. We are locally owned and family operated since 2012. If you are looking for friendly, professional movers who will take care of you valuables (and non-valuables) like our own, look no further. At Family Affairs Moving, we pride ourselves on our service, quality of work, and our affordable prices. We DO NOT offer trucks, but we do offer:

Services

  • Loading/Unloading of trucks, pods, and storage units
  • Packing/Unpacking

Rates
